About Us

NokshiBox is a simple online platform that connects Bangladeshi artisans with people looking for meaningful handmade gifts. We empower women, students, and home-based crafters to easily showcase and sell their creations. Our mission is to celebrate local craftsmanship, support small businesses, and make handmade gifting effortless and special. By bridging creators and customers, NokshiBox builds a supportive community where creativity thrives, culture is preserved, and every gift carries a personal story. Together, we aim to redefine gifting with authenticity, love, and sustainability.
